The Butler basketball team is from Butler University, which is located in Indianapolis, Indiana. It was founded in 1927. They have appeared in 16 NCAA tournaments and have won none of them. They have am amazing court called Hinkle Fieldhouse.

'

They are in the Big East conference, along with 63 other schools. Butler's basketball team has a very strong fanbase, with more fans than any of Butler's other sports.
